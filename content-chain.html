01

The Motivational Poster

One quote from your session. One image prompt. Ready to drop into ChatGPT and generate a shareable poster your audience will save, repost, and pin on their wall.

02

The Facebook Story Post

The post that stops the scroll. Not because it's clever — because it's true. The chain reads your session, finds the moment, and writes the story in your voice. Five hook options. You pick one. You post it.

03

The Instagram Caption

Same story. Right length for Instagram. Five hashtags. Done automatically from the Facebook post. Two platforms. One idea. Zero extra thinking.

04

Five Branded Story Slides

Your face, your colours, your CTA. Five slides ready to upload. Stories keep you visible every single day with the people who already follow you — and top of mind is what makes someone think of you when they're ready to buy.

05

The Quote Carousel

Ten quotes pulled from your session. Ten image prompts you take straight to ChatGPT or Canva. Carousels get saved — and saved posts keep working for weeks after you post them.

06

The Video Script

A full script built from your session. Hook, story, shift, close. Read-off ready. You hit record. One take. You post. No more sitting in front of a camera wondering what to say.
